Michael's Favorite Animal

Killer Whale

Killer Whales, also known by their scientific name Orcinus Orca are the top predator of the ocean. They live all around the world and live in pods led by a female member. While they have no known predators in the wild, humans have caused trouble for whales in the wild. Killer Whales use their black and white appearance to blend into deep water in order to hunt. Orca's use echolocation to communicate with other members of their pod and use behaviors like breaching and jumping to express feelings and enjoy a bit of play time!

For more information on Killer Whales visit SeaWorld.org
